## 📊 Business Problem
Greely Auto, an international automotive company, is planning a strategic entry into the US market. This project provides a data-backed roadmap to understand the pricing dynamics of the American automotive industry, enabling the company to position its launch models effectively.

## 🚀 Technical Highlights
- *Predictive Modeling:* Built a Decision Tree Regressor achieving *88.73% accuracy* in car price forecasting.
- *Interactive Visualization:* A 3-page Power BI dashboard designed for executive stakeholders:
    1. *Executive Overview:* Market share, brand distribution, and body style analysis.
    2. *Model Performance:* Deep dive into prediction accuracy vs. actual market prices.
    3. *AI Insights:* Automatic identification of price drivers (Engine Size, Horsepower, etc.).
- *Data Engineering:* Managed feature encoding and data normalization using Python.
